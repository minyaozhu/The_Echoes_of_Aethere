# **The Cloud Harvester: Teaser Trailer Script**

**Logline**: In a floating city above a toxic world, a scavenger finds proof that the earth below is green. Now she just has to survive the fall.

**Style**: Hyper-Realistic Sci-Fi. Gritty, atmospheric, high tension.
**Music**: Starts with a low, throbbing bass (Heartbeat). Builds to a high-pitched, intense synth crescendo.

---

## **Scene 1: The city in the Sky**
**Visual**: wide shot of **Aethere**. A massive, glittering city of glass and solar sails floating high above a thick layer of purple clouds. The sun is blindingly bright.
**Audio (SFX)**: High wind. The chiming of crystal wind chimes.
**Voiceover (Elara)**: "They told us the world died."

## **Scene 2: The Plunge**
**Visual**: **Elara** (in her suit) standing on the edge of a rusty platform. She leans forward and **drops**. The camera follows her falling *into* the purple clouds.
**Audio (SFX)**: Silence. Then a sudden *ROAR* of wind as she hits the cloud layer.
**Voiceover (Elara)**: "They said the ground was poison."

## **Scene 3: The Artifact**
**Visual**: Close up on Elara's hand in the dark fog. She is holding a **glowing amber capsule**. It pulses with a warm, golden light.
**Audio (SFX)**: A digital chime. *Ping... Ping... Ping...*
**Voiceover (Elara)**: "They lied."

## **Scene 4: The Betrayal**
**Visual**: Quick cuts.
1.  **Councilor Thorne** in his white robes, looking cold and terrifying.
2.  **Silas** slamming a heavy metal door shut, locking Elara in.
3.  **Kaelen** yelling, being dragged away by armored guards.
**Audio (SFX)**: Slamming door. Sirens wailing. Thorne saying: *"Hope is a virus."*

## **Scene 5: The Dive (Action Montage)**
**Visual**: The **Windrunner** (orange ship) diving vertically *through* a storm of violet lightning.
**Visual**: Elara in the cockpit, wrestling with the yoke, screaming.
**Visual**: A massive Black Interceptor ship firing green plasma bolts at them.
**Audio (Music)**: The synth music kicks in. Fast. Aggressive. *Dun-dun-dun-dun.*

## **Scene 6: The Green Valley**

**Voiceover (Elara)**: "Welcome home."

## **Scene 7: Title Card**
**Visual**: Text appears on black: **THE ECHOES OF AETHERE**
**Visual**: Subtext: **BOOK 1: THE CLOUD HARVESTER**
