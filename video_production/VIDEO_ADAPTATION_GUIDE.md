# **Video Adaptation Guide: The Cloud Harvester**

Transforming your book into a video is an ambitious and exciting project! Since you have the full manuscript, we can approach this like a true production studio.

## **Phase 1: Pre-Production (The Blueprint)**
Before you make a single frame, you need a plan.
1.  **Script Adaptation**: Novels don't translate directly to screen. We need to convert prose into a **Screenplay** (Scene Headings, Action Lines, Dialogue).
    -   *Example*: Instead of "Elara felt fear," the script says "CLOSE UP on Elara's eyes widening."
2.  **Visual Style**: Decide on the look.
    -   **Cinematic Realism**: Like a movie (Blade Runner meets Avatar).
    -   **Stylized Animation**: Like Arcane or Spider-Verse (2.5D, painted textures).
    -   **Anime**: Studio Ghibli or Cyberpunk Edgerunners style.
3.  **Audio Design**: Voice acting (ElevenLabs/OpenAI), Music (Suno/Udio), and SFX.

## **Phase 2: Asset Generation (The Building Blocks)**
This is where AI tools save the day.
-   **Visuals**:
    -   **Midjourney / Flux / DALL-E 3**: Generate keyframes (static images) for every shot. *I can help with this!*
    -   **Runway Gen-3 Alpha / Luma Dream Machine / Kling**: Animate those static images into 5-10 second video clips.
-   **Audio**:
    -   **ElevenLabs**: Generate voices for Elara, Kaelen, and Thorne.
    -   **Udio / Suno**: Compose a custom soundtrack (e.g., "Solarpunk synthwave with orchestral swells").

## **Phase 3: Post-Production (Assembly)**
-   **Editing**: Use Davinci Resolve (free & powerful) or CapCut (easy).
-   **Sync**: Match the video clips to the audio track.
-   **Lip Sync**: Tools like **Hallo** or **Hedra** can make your characters actually speak the dialogue.

## **Proposed Workflow: The "Teaser Trailer"**
Instead of trying to make the whole movie at once, let's start with a **60-second Teaser Trailer**.

**My Role (The Director/Writer):**
1.  **Write the Script**: A high-impact trailer script with voiceover and scene descriptions.
2.  **Generate Concept Art**: I will create the "Keyframes" for the trailer (Deep Cloud, The Windrunner, The Green Valley).
3.  **Write Video Prompts**: I will give you the exact text to paste into Luma/Runway to animate those keyframes.

**Your Role (The Producer):**
1.  **Generate the Video**: Use the prompts/images I give you in external tools.
2.  **Assemble**: Stitch the clips together.
